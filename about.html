What is Starforge AI?

The idea for Starforge AI emerged after noticing a growing need for AI in businesses. Many companies, especially small and medium-sized enterprises, struggle with repetitive tasks, inefficient workflows, and untapped opportunities for growth.

We saw that these businesses often lacked the resources or expertise to implement AI solutions, even though automation could dramatically improve their efficiency and scalability. Starforge AI was created to bridge this gap and make AI accessible, practical, and valuable for businesses of all sizes.
